Design is indispensable

Our life's easiest and most unnoticeable actions revolve around greatly designed products. This notion has intrigued me to explore the art of design, in building products that will benefit the user.

I currently work as a developer, with a desire to transition in to design. I have a keen eye for detail, which got me in to designing wide range of products from graphic art, clothing and now, web applications.

I am a self-taught designer based out of San Francisco and I continue to build side projects to individuals and small business owners.
